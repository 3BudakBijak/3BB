Health Tips for Kids

Balanced Diet

Ensure your child gets a balanced diet with plenty of fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.

Regular Exercise

Encourage at least 60 minutes of physical activity every day to keep kids fit and healthy.

Proper Hydration

Make sure your child drinks plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated.

Enough Sleep

Children need 9-11 hours of sleep each night to support their growth and development.
